Solved Technical FAQ

Search through frequent trouble tickets compiled from active subscribers.

What does the blinking red WAN light on the router mean?
A blinking red WAN light indicates that the router is not receiving an optical connection from the external fiber line. Check if the yellow fiber patch cord is plugged securely into the blue GPON port on the back. If secure, open a ticket for technician review.
How do I configure the WiFi network name (SSID) on Stackly routers?
Connect to your Stackly router via cable or wireless, open a web browser, and go to http://192.168.1.1. Enter the admin credentials printed on the router label, navigate to "Wireless Settings", and update the SSID and passphrase.
How do I test my connection latency/ping?
Open the command terminal on your computer and type "ping 8.8.8.8". A standard high-speed Stackly fiber connection averages a ping between 2ms to 8ms to public DNS nodes.
