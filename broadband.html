Broadband FAQ

Got questions about fiber installation, hardware setup, or billing? We've got answers.

Are there any hidden data caps or Fair Usage Policies (FUP)?

No. All Stackly Fiber Broadband plans offer 100% truly unlimited data without any hidden speed caps, data throttling, or monthly bandwidth ceilings.

How fast is the fiber installation process?

Once you confirm your location, our certified technician installs the optical network terminal (ONT) and WiFi-6 router within 24 to 48 hours.

Is the Wi-Fi 6 router equipment included for free?

Yes! Every Superfast and Gigabit broadband package includes a high-performance Dual-Band WiFi-6 Smart Router at zero extra equipment rental cost.
