FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to common questions about installation, billing, 5G devices, and custom setups.

How long does the fiber installation process take?
Standard fiber broadband installation takes 2-4 hours. Once you register a request, our field engineer completes site mapping and links the connection on the same day.
Is my older phone compatible with Stackly 5G?
Stackly 5G works on all major 5G-ready smartphones supporting sub-6GHz frequencies. If your phone supports older bands, it will fall back automatically to our premium 4G LTE network.
Do you throttle speed after a certain usage limit?
No. Our premium fiber broadband plans offer completely unlimited downloads and uploads with zero speed throttling or Fair Usage Policy (FUP) caps.
